About

 Welcome to Ott's Off-Leash Dog Sanctuary, a non-profit organization created by a love for dogs and a vision to provide excellence in canine recreation. -Pott's Dog Sanctuary is not for sale. In fact, it is being legally restructured to become a permanent fixture in our community.

What We Do

 Dr. Potts, along with a dedicated team of volunteers is working to maintain and improve the facilities. We are committed to creating a safe, clean, and fun environment for dogs and their owners.

Enjoy Your Visit

Please familiarize yourself with the rules outlined in the Regulations Section (link to sub-page) to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all visitors. The Sanctuary takes these rules seriously, and enforces them for the safety of both dogs and their owners.
